70 AD

Construction of the Colosseum began under the rule of Vespasian. Vespasian completed up to the third story by the time he died in 79. His son, Titus, finished the top level in year 80.

What else is happening?

The Roman Empire in 70 AD destroyed a temple in Jerusalem as a response to the Jewish comunity in Judea rebelling in 66 AD. The Romans killed thousands. The Romans took the treasure and gold and used that to build the Colosseum.

80 AD

The inaugural games were held in year 80. Durring the inaugural games Dio Cassius claims that over 9,000 wild animals were killed. Special coins were issued celebrating the inauguration

100 AD

The building was remodelled furhter under Vespasian's younger son, the newly designated Emperor Domitian. He Constructed the hypogeum; a series of underground tunnels used to house animals and slaves. He also added a gallery to the top of the Colosseum to increase the seating capacity.
